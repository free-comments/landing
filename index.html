Free Comments

Free.co is an innovative, social internet extension, a revolutionary platform designed to facilitate genuine, respectful, and free exchanges of opinion without the interference of commercial interests or automated systems. We strive to create a digital space where all voices can contribute to a vibrant and inclusive dialogue.

Free.co is currently shipped as Google chrome extension that adds a powerful tool with several functionalities to social media sites:

  1. 1 Ability to send signed comments to different content anonymously.
  2. 2 Blocking of commercial ads.
  3. 3 Ability to create your own feed with notifications based on content eligible for free comments.
